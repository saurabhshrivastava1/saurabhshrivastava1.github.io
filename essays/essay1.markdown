    <h2 style="font-style: italic; margin-bottom: 5px;">The Value of Minimalist Design</h2>
    <p style="color: #888; font-size: 12px; margin-bottom: 30px;">January 2026</p>

    <p>The early web was characterized by a lack of ornament. Before the rise of heavy frameworks and auto-playing videos, the internet was a place for shared text and simple ideas.</p>

    <p>Building a site in the style of the early web—or the modern minimalist "Stripe-adjacent" aesthetic—is an exercise in constraint. By limiting the design to two columns and a single serif font, we force the focus back onto the content itself.</p>

    <p>This is my first experiment in that direction.</p>
